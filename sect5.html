The Bloodbinding

To call upon forces best left undisturbed.

  1. I Draw the sigil in your own blood upon virgin parchment.
  2. II Whisper the Words of Unmaking three times under a waning moon.
  3. III Burn the parchment and inhale the smoke.
  4. IV Sleep with the ashes beneath your tongue.

The price is paid in dreams.

Rite of the Hollow Vein

The Silent Pact

For those who bargain in whispers.

  1. I Carve the name of your desire into a candle of black wax.
  2. II Light it at the stroke of midnight, speaking no words.
  3. III Let it burn until dawn, extinguishing it with bare fingers.
  4. IV Bury the remains where no light touches.

They will come within seven nights.
