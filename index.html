Understanding our mission, ethics, and origins.

Our Story

Arc71 was founded in 2013 by a collective of cybersecurity researchers, privacy advocates, and ethical hackers who believed that digital privacy is a fundamental human right.

What began as a small community of like-minded individuals has grown into one of the most respected resources on the darknet for cybersecurity tools and knowledge.

Our Mission

We believe in a future where individuals have control over their digital lives. Our mission is to develop and provide tools that empower people to protect their privacy, secure their communications, and defend against surveillance.

We operate on the principle that security knowledge should be accessible to all, not just those with resources or connections.

Ethics Statement

Arc71 is committed to ethical hacking principles. We do not condone using our tools for malicious purposes, financial gain through illegal activities, or attacks against critical infrastructure.

All our tools are developed for educational purposes, security research, and defensive cybersecurity practices.

The Team

Our team consists of cybersecurity professionals from around the world. For security reasons, we maintain anonymity but we're united by our commitment to digital rights and privacy.

We include cryptographers, network engineers, software developers, and security researchers who contribute to the project.
